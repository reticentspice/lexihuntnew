About Lexihunt


Your goal in Lexihunt is to type a word worth exactly points. The catch is that you don't know how many points each letter is worth—just that it's between 1 and 7. Guess strategically to find letter values and make a word whose value matches the target number above.


Game design: Chris Backe
Scripting/web design: Laura Kingdon

Rules

  • Type a 3-8 letter word into the box labeled 'Guess', then hit 'Enter'.
  • You'll see the total points that word is worth and a hint about the value of one letter.
  • You have a maximum of 10 guesses. Each guess is color-coded according to how close you are: red is way off, orange is closer, and yellow is very close.
  • When you find a word worth points, you win! Click the 'Share' button to brag about it on social media.

Tips

  • Feel free to use paper and pen to take notes, or use the notepad on the page.
  • It may help to look back at previous guesses to infer what a letter is worth.
  • If you're playing on a phone, we recommend turning your phone sideways to use the on-screen keyboard, or disabling it and using your phone keyboard.
  • Our word list contains the vast majority of common English words.
  • This is the alpha version and still contains some bugs. We apologize for the inconvenience. Please do let us know on social media if you encounter any serious issues.

Versions

  • Daily Lexihunt is the same for everyone, and you can play it only once a day. Feel free to share it on social media to show off!
  • Free Lexihunt is like Daily Lexihunt, but you can play it as much as you like! You can't share your results, though...
  • Easy Lexihunt is like Free Lexihunt, but you can see the letter values.
  • And Hard Lexihunt has higher numbers, so you need to make longer words to win.
  • Note that the puzzles for all of these games are different! Sadly, you can't reuse your winning word from Easy to beat the Daily puzzle in one go...
